General Acounting



Accounting is essential to be able to grow business in a way that can be measured and predicted. Having a system of tracking business' assets, liabilities, and income helps to make smart, informed business decisions based on the past performance and present financial health of the organisation. Sound accounting practices and a clear, organized accounting system helps knowing where the Organisation stands financially and also address the statutory and management needs and also provide information to the various stakeholders of business.

Providing financial information to our clients in a timely and accurate manner is a commitment that we feel cannot be compromised. The service involves preparing and maintaining day to day bookkeeping and monthly or quarterly management accounts. These books are prepared as per the relevant accounting standards and can also be made as per specific client instructions.

We use the latest technology and provide online accounting services to our clients. We also provide the accounting services on a variety of Accounting platforms suited to the client needs.

General ledger and financial statement preparation Accounting system setup for new businesses Computerized payroll services Bookkeeping Business tax return preparation Personal financial statements Deployment of Accounting Resources The system chosen should integrate as many of the clients business processes as possible to save time and minimize data entry errors.
